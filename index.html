Who I Am

I am a seasoned State Tested Nurse Aide turned Softward Developer. I am the mother and wife to my very own cheerleading squad. I love solving problems and I am a fixer. Healthcare has allowed me to do that for over the past 10+ years that I have been taking care of people. Over the years I have noticed how much of my job changed due to technology. The once written, became electronic charting. Daily processes were becoming efficient due to becoming computerized. I grew more and more curious, and I had to know more about the technology that I was now using daily. Being a mother and wife has made me extremely efficient, motivated and able to multitask. All of these reasons brought me to my decision of learning to code.
